About MSU

The Stanford Muslim Student Union is a vibrant, welcoming community of Muslim students at Stanford University. We are dedicated to nurturing faith, fostering genuine connection, and serving those around us.

From weekly Jumu'ah prayers and Quran study circles to interfaith dialogues and community iftars, our programming spans the full breadth of Muslim campus life.

Whether you are a practicing Muslim, a student exploring Islam, or simply looking for community — you belong here.
